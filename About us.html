Our Interesting Story

A strong affection for traditional Kasi food and a desire to spread this love to as many people as possible gave birth to Kasi Meals. Our quest started with a straightforward yet deep idea: to distill the flavor of traditional Kasi meals and serve them in a way that pleases modern palates while paying tribute to their heritage. The concept of Kasi Meals is based on our founder, Thato Manyama, who was raised surrounded by the fragrances of home-cooked Kasi foods. Our business was founded on February 18, 2018. With the lively culinary culture of Kasi and family recipes as our inspiration, we set out to create a location where every meal tells a story and every mouthful makes you happy.

Our Vision

At Kasi Meals, we think that food has the ability to unite people. In order to make every dish more than just a dinner, but rather a celebration of flavor and heritage, we take great pleasure in utilizing only the freshest, locally sourced ingredients. Our dishes are thoughtfully created, combining classic methods with a dash of creativity to provide a dining experience that is thrilling and real. We are dedicated to promoting sustainability and aiding the neighborhood. We guarantee the best quality and promote the welfare of our community by obtaining products from nearby farmers and producers.
